About

The pink city of India

History

My city Jaipur was founded in the year 1727. The city has many historic memories and it is amongst the most beautiful cities in India. The city was constructed according to the Vastu Shashtra and also Shilpa Shastra. It has a very rich legacy and people of Jaipur still follow the legacy. The palaces and the Ranis and the Maharajas of Jaipur have left their legacies. The city was painted pink to welcome the Prince of Wales under the rule of Swami Ram Singh and since then the city was also known as the Pink City.

Architecture

The Pink City – Jaipur is built according to the Vastu Shastra, which was given by Vidyadhar Bhattacharya. There are three gates in the city facing East, West, and North. The eastern gate is known as Suraj Pol, the western as Chand Pol and the Northern gates are facing the capital of Amer. The city has a lot of palaces and rich heritage. The Hawa Mahal is very famous across the country. There is also the Nahargarh Fort, which also the residential place of the King Sawai Jai Singh II.

Culture

The city is also famous for its gemstones and people across the world prefer buying stones from this city. The people of Jaipur speak Rajasthani, Hindi, Marwari, Dhundari, and English. Marwari is the most commonly spoken language of the city and even of the state. Jaipur has a famous literature festival that is famous across the world and poets, writers and people who love literature participate in this festival. The other festivals include Gangaur, Teej, Kite Festival and Elephant festival.
